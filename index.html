About Me

I'm a Senior Magento Developer and Full-Stack IT Professional with over 21 years of experience in the Technology, Information, and Internet industry. Specializing in Magento e-commerce, I build scalable, performance-optimized solutions with seamless workflows and exceptional user experiences. My expertise includes custom Magento 2 module development, third-party integrations (Mailchimp, ShipperHQ, custom APIs), ERP syncing, and building platforms with 99.9% uptime. I'm proficient in both Hyvä and Luma themes, ensuring modern, responsive frontends tailored to each client's needs.

Beyond development, I bring strong DevOps and infrastructure experience including; CI/CD automation, AWS (EC2, S3, CodeCommit), Proxmox, Bash scripting, and Git versioning. I'm also experienced in payment gateway integration, server management, and cross-functional collaboration to meet business goals. Over my career, I've been a developer, manager, architect, marketer, support tech, and even an electrician and logistics coordinator giving me a uniquely holistic view of problem-solving and systems thinking. I thrive in multidisciplinary roles and adapt quickly to project demands.
