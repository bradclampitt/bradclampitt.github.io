A Little About Me

While I'm passionate about technology and building amazing digital experiences, there's so much more to life than code and servers. I believe that being well-rounded and having diverse interests makes me a better developer, colleague, and person.

When I'm not deep in a Magento module or optimizing server performance, you'll find me spending quality time with my family, exploring new places, or diving into one of my many hobbies. I'm a firm believer that life experiences and personal connections fuel creativity and problem-solving skills in unexpected ways.

I love meeting new people and hearing their stories. Whether we're discussing the latest web technologies or sharing tales from our latest adventures, I'm always eager to connect on both professional and personal levels.

Family First

Family is the foundation of everything I do. My wife and twin boys are my biggest supporters and my greatest motivation. They keep me grounded and remind me what's truly important in life.

We love spending time together whether it's family game nights, exploring new places, or just enjoying quiet evenings at home. They're incredibly patient with my tech obsessions and even show genuine interest when I get excited about a new framework or successful deployment!

Being a parent has taught me patience, creativity, and the importance of clear communication - skills that translate directly into being a better developer and team member.
